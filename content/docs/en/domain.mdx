<Callout type="info">
To use your custom domain, you need to configure your DNS records to point to our server.
</Callout>

---

## Step 1: Access DNS Management

Log in to your domain provider (GoDaddy, Namecheap, OVH, etc.), then open:

> **DNS Management**

---

## Step 2: Add DNS Records

You must add **two required DNS records**:

| Type  | Name (Host) | Value           |
|-------|------------|-----------------|
| A     | @          | 66.29.149.126   |
| CNAME | www        | moneyfusion.net |

### Explanation

- **A (@)** → connects your root domain (`yourdomain.com`) to our server  
- **CNAME (www)** → redirects `www.yourdomain.com` to your root domain  

<Callout type="warning">
Remove any existing <strong>@</strong> and <strong>www</strong> records before adding the new ones.
</Callout>

---

## Step 3: Wait for DNS Propagation

<Callout type="info">
DNS propagation can take from a few minutes up to <strong>24 hours</strong>, and in some cases up to <strong>48 hours</strong>.
</Callout>

---

## External Verification

👉 https://dnschecker.org

---

## Step 4: Verify and Add Your Domain

1. Log in to your **MoneyFusion** dashboard  
2. Go to **Add Domain**  
3. Enter your domain (`yourdomain.com`)  
4. Click **"Verify my domain"**

---

## Result

- ✅ Domain validated  
- ✅ Automatically connected to your store  

<Callout type="success">
The system automatically checks that your domain points to <strong>66.29.149.126</strong>.
</Callout>

---

## Common Issues

- Incorrect IP address in the **A record**  
- Missing or incorrect **CNAME www**  
- Conflict with existing DNS records  
- DNS propagation not completed
